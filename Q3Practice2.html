BLAME!

BLAME! is a cyberpunk manga series written and illustrated by Tsutomu Nihei. It is set in a dystopian future where the City, an ever expanding megastructure that stretches well beyond Earth's atmosphere, is inhabited by artificial intelligences.

Character Image Description
Killy Killy is the enigmatic main protagonist of BLAME! by Tsutomu Nihei. Armed with the mighty GBE, he is a provisional Safeguard unit tasked with finding the Net Terminal Gene, which, if found, would be able to halt the expansion of the City, thereby saving all human life within it from hostility by the rogue Safeguards.
Cibo Cibo (SEE-boh) was the head scientist of the Bio-Electric Corporation. Killy finds Cibo as a robotic torso and head in the ruins under the Electrofisher town. Once found and resurrected with the help of Killy, she becomes his sole true ally in his quest to find the Net Terminal Gene.
Sanakan Sanakan (Sana-Kan) is first seen as a short, young girl with black hair who finds Killy and Cibo in a tunnel after they pierce the impenetrable Megastructure layer with the GBE. However, Killy later discovers her true nature, and she reveals herself as a level 6 Safeguard with a GBE similar to Killy's. At first, Sana-Kan behaves like any Safeguard intent on annihilating all humans without the Net Terminal Gene.
